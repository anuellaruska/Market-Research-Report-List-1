<p><ul><li>Conventional Media</li><li>Coalescing Media</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Coalescing Plate Oil Water Separator market is categorized into two main types: Conventional Media and Coalescing Media. Conventional Media separators utilize gravity to separate oil and water, relying on traditional filtration methods. In contrast, Coalescing Media separators enhance separation efficiency by using specialized coalescing plates or media that facilitate the merging of smaller oil droplets into larger ones, promoting faster and more effective separation. Both types aim to improve water quality by removing contaminants effectively from wastewater streams.</p></p>
