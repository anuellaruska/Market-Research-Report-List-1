<p><p>The MEMS Differential Pressure Sensor market is segmented into digital and analog types. Digital differential pressure sensors utilize microelectromechanical systems (MEMS) technology to provide precise, reliable measurements in a digital format, enhancing accuracy and ease of integration with digital systems. Conversely, analog differential pressure sensors deliver continuous voltage or current signals proportional to pressure differences, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for various applications. Both types cater to industries like HVAC, automotive, and medical, addressing diverse operational requirements.</p></p>
