<p><strong>The Furniture Varnish Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Waterborne</li><li>Solventborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The furniture varnish market is primarily divided into two types: waterborne and solventborne varnishes. Waterborne varnishes utilize water as the primary carrier, offering low VOC emissions and easier cleanup, making them environmentally friendly. They dry quickly and provide a clear finish, but may require multiple coats for durability. Solventborne varnishes, on the other hand, use organic solvents, resulting in a more robust and durable finish. They typically offer better resistance to moisture and wear but come with higher VOC emissions and longer drying times.</p></p>
