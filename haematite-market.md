<p><strong>The Haematite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lump</li><li>Pellet</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The haematite market is categorized into three main types: lump, pellet, and powder. Lump haematite consists of naturally occurring ore used in iron production, offering high iron content and minimal processing. Pellets are produced from powdered haematite, agglomerated into rounded balls, enhancing iron extraction efficiency in high-temperature furnaces. Powdered haematite is finely ground for applications in pigments, coatings, and as a chemical feedstock, showcasing versatility in various industries. Each form serves distinct functions and meets specific demands within the market.</p></p>
