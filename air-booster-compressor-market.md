<p><strong>The Air Booster Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric</li><li>Dynamic</li><li>Thermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Booster Compressor Market encompasses three primary types: Volumetric, Dynamic, and Thermal. Volumetric compressors utilize pistons or diaphragms to compress air at fixed volumes, making them suitable for various industrial applications. Dynamic compressors, such as centrifugal types, use rotating blades to compress air continuously, providing high flow rates and efficiency. Thermal compressors leverage temperature changes to enhance air pressure, often employing heat exchangers. Each type serves distinct needs, impacting efficiency, capacity, and operational costs in diverse sectors.</p></p>
