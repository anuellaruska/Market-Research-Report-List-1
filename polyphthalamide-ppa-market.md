Amorphous PPA offers excellent transparency and impact resistance, making it suitable for applications in electronics and automotive components. Semi-crystalline PPA provides enhanced chemical resistance and stiffness, ideal for demanding environments in industrial and aerospace sectors. Together, these types serve diverse applications, catering to the needs of various industries.</p></p>
