Zeolite catalysts, with their unique porous structures, facilitate specific reactions in petrochemical and environmental applications. The "other" category includes diverse catalysts such as organocatalysts and enzymes, which offer alternative pathways for chemical transformations. Each type plays a vital role in optimizing production processes across various industries.</p></p>
