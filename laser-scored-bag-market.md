<p><p>The laser scored bag market comprises several types based on scoring techniques. Inline laser scoring utilizes continuous scoring along the production line for efficient bag creation. Transverse laser scoring emphasizes horizontal lines, ensuring easy tearing. Freeform laser scoring allows for custom designs, enhancing brand appeal. Micro-perforation scoring involves tiny holes that enable easy opening while maintaining bag integrity. Other methods might include varied scoring patterns and techniques tailored to specific applications, broadening the functionality and usability of laser scored bags.</p></p>
