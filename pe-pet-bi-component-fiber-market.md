<p><p>The PE & PET bi-component fiber market is divided into two main types: staple fiber and filament. Staple fiber is characterized by short lengths, primarily used in textiles for applications like clothing and home furnishings, providing warmth and softness. In contrast, filament fiber consists of continuous strands, offering strength and durability, making it suitable for industrial applications and high-performance textiles. Both fiber types leverage the unique properties of polyethylene (PE) and polyethylene terephthalate (PET) for enhanced functionality and versatility in various markets.</p></p>
