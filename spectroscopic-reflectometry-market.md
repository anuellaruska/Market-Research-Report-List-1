<p><ul><li>Automatic Spectral Ellipsometer</li><li>Imaging Ellipsometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spectroscopic Reflectometry market is characterized by two main types: Automatic Spectral Ellipsometers and Imaging Ellipsometers. Automatic Spectral Ellipsometers are sophisticated devices that measure the change in polarization of reflected light to analyze thin films, providing precise optical property data. Meanwhile, Imaging Ellipsometers combine elliptical polarization analysis with imaging techniques, allowing for spatially resolved measurements of sample surfaces. Both types are essential in fields like materials science, semiconductor manufacturing, and nanotechnology for quality control and research purposes.</p></p>
