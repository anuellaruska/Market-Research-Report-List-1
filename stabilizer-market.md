<p><p>Stabilizer market types include:</p><p>1. **Anti-rolling Tank**: Utilizes water movement to counteract vessel rolling.</p><p>2. **Bilge Keel**: Fixed fins attached to the hull, reducing rolling motion by disrupting water flow.</p><p>3. **Anti-rolling Fin**: Retractable fins that adjust to counteract rolling forces dynamically.</p><p>4. **Gyrostabilizer**: Uses a spinning rotor to create angular momentum, stabilizing the vessel.</p><p>5. **Other**: Encompasses emerging technologies and hybrid systems aimed at enhancing stability on watercraft.</p></p>
