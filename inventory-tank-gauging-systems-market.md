<p><p>Inventory tank gauging systems are essential for managing liquid storage, divided into two primary market types: point level instruments and continuous level instruments. </p><p>Point level instruments detect specific liquid levels, triggering alarms or actions when thresholds are reached, ensuring safety and preventing overflows. In contrast, continuous level instruments provide real-time data on liquid levels, enabling precise monitoring and optimization of storage operations. Together, these instruments enhance efficiency, safety, and inventory management in various industries, including oil and gas, chemicals, and water treatment.</p></p>
