<p><p>The embedded security chips and modules market encompasses various technologies designed to enhance device security. Embedded Secure Elements (eSE) provide secure storage and processing capabilities for sensitive applications like payments and identity verification. Embedded SIM (eSIM) technology allows secure and remotely programmable mobile connectivity without physical SIM cards. Trusted Platform Modules (TPM) offer hardware-based security functions to protect devices against unauthorized access and ensure the integrity of hardware and software. Together, these solutions are vital for securing connected devices in an increasingly digital world.</p></p>
