<p><p>Wet abrasive paper, also known as wet sandpaper, is a type of coated abrasive material designed for use with water or other lubricants to reduce friction and prevent clogging during sanding. The water or lubricant helps to minimize dust, extend the paper's lifespan, and achieve a smoother finish.</p><p>The Wet Abrasive Paper Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for precision finishing in various industries and the rising adoption of advanced materials.
