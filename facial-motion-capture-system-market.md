<p><p>The Facial Motion Capture System market encompasses three primary types: </p><p>1. **Magnetic Motion Capture** utilizes magnetic sensors to track facial movement through a series of electromagnetic fields, providing accurate data in real-time. </p><p>2. **Mechanical Motion Capture** employs physical markers or mechanical devices attached to the face to capture movement through articulated components, ensuring precision in the details. </p><p>3. **Optical Motion Capture** relies on cameras and infrared light to track reflective markers on the face, offering high-resolution data and flexibility in various environments.</p></p>
